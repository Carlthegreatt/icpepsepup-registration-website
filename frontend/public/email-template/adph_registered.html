Registration

Time: 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM

Location: Humbon Gate

Late Registration: APC Cafeteria

Instructions:

  • Upon registration, please locate the table corresponding to the first letter of your surname and present your ticket to the registration officer.
  • Your ID may be requested for verification purposes.
  • Workshop registration is first-come, first-served.

Dress Code (For Non-APC Students)

Acceptable Tops: T-shirts, polo shirts, collared shirts, blouses

Acceptable Bottoms: Jeans, chinos, joggers, leggings, jeggings

Acceptable Footwear: Rubber shoes, topsiders, sneakers, loafers

Prohibited Attire:

  • Pedal pushers / capri pants
  • Slippers or flip-flops
  • Platform shoes
  • Skirts with excessively high hemlines or slits
  • Hats and beanies during seminars/workshops
  • Clothing with offensive words or images
  • Backless, see-through, or midriff-exposing tops
  • Tank tops or spaghetti straps without outerwear
